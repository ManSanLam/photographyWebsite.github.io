Hi, I’m Man San Lam

I started photography in the summer of 2025 and quickly got hooked on capturing the world around me. I mainly shoot street, architecture, and landscape photography because I love how each one shows a different side of everyday life. Most of my shots are taken in New York City, Chicago, Miami, and San Diego. Photography has become one of my favorite ways to explore new places and freeze little moments that often go unnoticed.
